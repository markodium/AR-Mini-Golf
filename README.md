# AR Mini Golf made in Unity using AR Foundation

Collaborative effort of Marko Dimchevski and Dario Sofreski

To open our app, download the **ARMiniGolf.apk** file and install it on an Android device that supports ARCore. Launch the application, scan a flat surface, place the golf course, and enjoy the game.

The application uses Augmented Reality plane detection to place virtual mini golf courses into the real world.

## The Rules:

The objective of the game is to complete each mini golf course by getting the golf ball into the hole.

After launching the application:

* Scan a flat surface such as a table or floor.
* Tap on the detected surface to place the golf course.
* Drag on the screen to aim and control the strength of your shot.
* Release to shoot the ball.
* Complete the level by getting the ball into the hole.
* Press **Next Level** to continue to the next course.
* Complete all available levels to finish the game.

Each level introduces a different layout and challenge, requiring players to adjust their aim and shot power to successfully reach the hole.
